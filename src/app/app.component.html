Mortgage Calculator Information:

The Fazzie May Mortgage Calculator© displays the payment breakdown for a standard fixed-rate mortgage based on user inputs. Mortgage calculators can play a vital role in the home buying process. They can also be fun to play with. The more informed home buyers are, the less likely they are to default. This results in lower delinquency rates for Fannie Mae and MBS investors. The calculator is made from Angular, HTML, CSS, TS, Chart.js, and Materialize running on an AWS EC2 instance. Created for the 2018 Fannie Mae Immersion Program.

Monthly Payment Formula:

M = P * ( ( r * ( 1 + r )^n ) / ( ( 1 + r )^n – 1 ) )

P - Principle, r - Interest Rate, n - Number of Payments

Total Payments Formula:

T = M * n

M - Monthly Payments, n - Number of Payments

Total Interest Payments Formula:

I = T - P

T - Total Payments, P - Principle
